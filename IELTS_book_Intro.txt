The Science of the Classroom
Inside the classroom, sitting at desks makes students sleepy and allows them to hide in the back row. Instead, Gemini selected specific, well-researched teaching methodologies to build real English fluency:

1. "Thinking Classrooms" (Standing Up to Learn): You will stand up and work in small groups on big wall whiteboards. Science calls this Embodied Cognition—using your physical body to wake up your brain. Standing gives you energy, and because whiteboard markers erase easily, you never have to fear making mistakes!

2. The 5-3-2 Vocabulary Rule (Spaced Repetition): We use a "Recursive Lexical Strategy." Every week, you learn exactly 5 topic words, 3 native idioms, and 2 recycled words from past weeks. This locks the English into your long-term memory forever.

3. Idea Building (Schema Activation): Many students freeze in tests because they run out of ideas. We use short videos to give you background ideas, then teach you to draw visual mind maps on your whiteboards. This builds "Narrative Endurance"—the stamina to speak easily for 2 full minutes.

4. Harvard "Thinking Routines": For difficult IELTS Part 3 questions, we use methods created by Harvard University. We teach you a simple formula: O.R.E. (Opinion, Reason, Example). This gives your brain a map to build deep, intelligent answers without stopping.

5. Instant 1-on-1 Coaching (Micro-Conferencing): Your teacher will constantly walk around the room to give you fast, 2-minute private coaching sessions. We fix your mistakes the exact moment you make them!
