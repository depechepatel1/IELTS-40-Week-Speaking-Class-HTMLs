Solving the "Memorization" Problem

In Chinese schools, students are usually excellent at reading and grammar but lack confidence in speaking. Because they are afraid of making mistakes, many students try to pass speaking exams by writing down perfect answers and memorizing them. However, IGCSE and IELTS examiners are trained to spot memorization and will give very low scores for it. Real fluency means thinking on your feet.
This course is specially designed to stop you from memorizing. In class, we give you a Model Answer, but we teach you to use it as a "menu" of good vocabulary, not a script to copy. In your handout's "Writing Space," you are only allowed to write short keywords, never full sentences. This forces your brain to speak naturally!

This guarantees you are building real, independent fluency!

The Science of Learning

Inside the classroom, we do not use old-fashioned repetition. Instead, we use five researched teaching methods proven to help Chinese learners get high scores:
  1. The Spiral Curriculum (Learn it Twice): We do not just study a topic once. In the first 20 weeks, we cover all 20 of the Cambridge topics to build your basic speaking skills. In the last 20 weeks, we "spiral" back and study the exact same 20 topics again, but this time using advanced grammar and deeper thinking.
  2. Memory Warm-ups (Spaced Retrieval): Science shows we forget new words quickly if we do not use them. Every lesson begins with a fun warm-up that forces you to remember and use words from the previous week. This locks the English vocabulary permanently into your long-term memory.
  3. Step-by-Step Grammar (Scaffolding): We build your skills slowly so you never feel stressed. In the early weeks, we help you add just one complex sentence to your answers. As you get stronger, we safely guide you to use two.
  4. The 4-3-2 Fluency Game: Many students translate Chinese to English in their heads, which makes speaking very slow. In this classroom game, you speak your original answer to a partner in 4 minutes, then 3 minutes, then 2 minutes. This trains your brain to stop translating and start speaking fast.
  5. Real Conversation Skills (Interactive Turn-Taking): The real exam tests if you can hold a natural conversation. We teach you exactly how to take turns, listen actively, and politely ask for help just like a native speaker (for example, learning to ask, "Sorry, did you mean...?").
By combining every topic from the world-class Cambridge textbook, amazing AI technology, and the best teaching science, you are getting the absolute best speaking course possible. We are excited to guide you to success!

科学教学法

课堂内，我们摒弃传统机械重复，采用五项经实证有效且专为中国学习者设计的教学策略：
  1. 螺旋式课程（双重强化）：每个主题经历两轮学习。前20周完成剑桥全主题基础口语构建；后20周以更高阶语法和深度思维重新研习相同主题，实现能力跃升。
  2. 记忆激活（间隔提取）：科学表明新词如不运用会快速遗忘。每节课以趣味活动启动，强制调用上周词汇，将英语表达永久锚定于长时记忆。
  3. 阶梯式语法（支架教学）：循序渐进培养复杂句式运用能力。初期阶段，我们引导学生在回答中嵌入一个复合句；随着能力提升，逐步拓展至两个复合句的安全运用。
  4. 4-3-2流利度挑战：针对“中译英”思维导致语速迟缓的痛点，通过课堂配对练习——分别用4分钟、3分钟、2分钟陈述同一观点，训练大脑实现英语直输。
  5. 真实对话技巧（互动话轮转换）：模拟真实考场中的自然交流场景，专项训练话轮转换、主动聆听、得体求助等母语者必备技能（例如学习使用“抱歉，您是指...？”等策略）。
