Q1: What are the main differences between living in a city and the countryside?

Opinion It seems to me that the main difference is the pace of life. Reason While cities are bustling and fast-paced, the countryside is generally peaceful and slow. Living in the city offers more job opportunities and amenities, whereas the countryside offers cleaner air and a stronger sense of community. Example For instance, in my hometown, people are always rushing, but in my grandmother's village, life is very relaxed.
  • Ideas: Location & Atmosphere (Vibrant/Quiet).
  • Famous for (Food, History, Scenery).
  • Facilities (Transport, Entertainment).
  • Feelings (Pride, Nostalgia, Belonging).
