📌 CUE CARD: Describe a time you received good service from a staff member in a shop/company.

You should say: Where the shop/company is, When you received the good service, What service you received, And explain how you felt about the service.

🏆 Band 6.5 Model Answer

To begin with, I want to talk about a recent shopping trip, which was incredibly efficient (高效的), and where I received impeccable (无懈可击的 / 完美的) service. Honestly, the shop assistant was extremely courteous (礼貌的) and attentive (体贴的 / 殷勤的) to my needs. Actually, she decided to go above and beyond (尽心尽力) to find my exact shoe size. Furthermore, she was quite persuasive (有说服力的) without being overly pushy. Throughout the process, she helped me with a smile (面带微笑). Consequently, I felt absolutely satisfied (满意的) with my purchase. Truly, they managed to treat someone like royalty (奉若上宾) in that store. Finally, I left feeling deeply appreciative (感激的) for her wonderful help.
💡 Analysis: Look for the Red sentence (3 clauses) and Blue words (Transitions).
