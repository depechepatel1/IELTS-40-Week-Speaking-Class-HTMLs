📌 CUE CARD: Describe a family member who you are proud of.

You should say: Who this person is, When this happened, What this person did, And explain why you felt proud.

🏆 Band 6.5 Model Answer

To begin with, I want to talk about my uncle, who is an accomplished (有造诣的 / 成功的) doctor, because I truly look up to (敬仰) him. Actually, he is a very diligent (勤奋的) person who always puts others first. Whenever his patients need help, he is considerate (体贴的) and completely selfless (无私的). In fact, he has a generous (慷慨的) nature and essentially has a heart of gold (心地善良). For example, he will always go the extra mile (加倍努力) to ensure (确保) everyone is safe. I think, seeing his devoted (挚爱的 / 忠诚的) attitude is highly inspiring (鼓舞人心的) to me. To be honest, I felt so proud when he saved a life last year.
💡 Analysis: Look for the Red sentence (3 clauses) and Blue words (Transitions).

Q5: How does family pride contribute to the stability of a society?

Op It is widely acknowledged that, family pride creates a strong foundation, which ultimately leads to a more stable community. Re The core reason is that, people naturally want to protect and support their own flesh and blood (骨肉 / 亲人). Ex For instance, families that respect each other are far less likely to commit crimes (犯罪) in public areas.
  • Encourages mutual support
  • Reduces poverty levels
  • Sharing financial burdens
Your Bullet Point Notes:
📉 Band 5 Peer Check: Ask: 'How does a happy family help the neighborhood?'
📈 Band 6 Peer Check: Ask: 'Can too much family pride cause problems with others?'

Q6: Do you think the definition of "success" within families has changed between the past and the present?

Op Undeniably, the definition of success has evolved (演变), as modern families value personal happiness over just financial wealth. Re In the past, success simply meant being the main breadwinner (养家糊口的人) to provide basic necessities (必需品). Ex Today, however, achieving a healthy work-life balance and pursuing individual passions are seen as equally important.
  • Yes significantly shifted
  • More dual incomes
  • Mothers working globally
